Frequently asked questions

Will I have the chance to interact with the elephants?

Guests observe the rescued elephants, feed them along a 400-meter skywalk after a food demonstration, and accompany them to a mud pit. This structured interaction allows you to appreciate the elephants’ behavior in an observant and relaxed setting.

How does the hotel pickup work?

Hotel pickup and drop-off are arranged at the chosen location in Koh Samui. The activity provider schedules the pickup based on your booking details, ensuring a smooth and seamless start and end to the tour.

What guide language options are available?

Guides are provided in Chinese, English, and Thai. A German-speaking guide is also offered on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Sunday. Your guide will share insights about the sanctuary while ensuring the tour flows naturally.

What activities can I expect during the tour?

The tour begins with an introductory session on elephant history followed by a demonstration on preparing nutritious food for the elephants. Guests will feed the elephants on the skywalk, observe them in a playful mud pit, and enjoy a premium buffet lunch.
