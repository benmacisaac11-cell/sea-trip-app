🛂 Arrival Cards — Cheat-Sheet

Three countries, three free digital cards. Each opens a 3-day window before arrival. Solo traveller — one QR each, emailed to you. Show the QR at immigration.

⚠️ All three are 100% FREE. There are dozens of copycat sites that charge a "fee" or harvest your passport data — they're often the top Google results (ads). Only use the exact official URLs below — bookmark them now. If an official site is down, try a different browser.

1st🇮🇩 Indonesia — All Indonesia Arrival Card ✅ SUBMITTED

One unified form = immigration arrival + customs (old e-CD) + health declaration. Separate from your eVOA visa (already done ✅).

Submit windowWed 1 Jul → 4 Jul (within 72h before arrival) — do it before you leave Perth

Arrival card #2607020021421 · submitted 2 Jul 2026
→ allindonesia.imigrasi.go.id 📎 View my QR (local only)

Returns a QR by email. Have it ready at DPS immigration + the customs lane. QR image kept local only (Confirmations/) — not part of the public app build, since it carries your passport number.

2nd🇸🇬 Singapore — SG Arrival Card (SGAC)

Includes the electronic health declaration. False declarations are an offence — keep it honest.

Arrive onScoot TR289 · Denpasar → Changi (lands 16:55) · Tue 14 Jul 2026
Submit windowSat 11 Jul → 14 Jul (within 3 days incl. arrival day) — do it from Bali
1st-night addressHeritage Collection on Chinatown · 227 South Bridge Road, Singapore 058787
Official siteeservices.ica.gov.sg/sgarrivalcard (or MyICA app)
→ eservices.ica.gov.sg/sgarrivalcard

✈️ Flying home (2–3 Aug): TR649 USM→SIN→MEL only transits Changi airside — you don't clear Singapore immigration, so no SGAC needed for the trip home.

3rd🇹🇭 Thailand — TDAC (Digital Arrival Card)

Mandatory for all foreigners since May 2025. Replaces the old paper TM6 card.

Arrive onSingapore Airlines SQ726 · Singapore → Phuket (lands 08:35) · Sat 18 Jul 2026
Submit windowWed 15 Jul → 18 Jul (within 72h before arrival) — do it from Singapore
1st-night addressStay Wellbeing & Lifestyle Resort · 116/29 Soi Saiyuan 10, Tambon Rawai, Phuket 83130 · +66 76 380 333

⚠️ Traveller tip: don't set your departure date = arrival date — the form then assumes you're transiting and greys out the accommodation field. Returns a QR by email.

📋 Have these handy for all three

  • Passport — number + expiry (✔ check 6+ months validity past 2 Aug)
  • Arrival flight number — listed on each card above
  • First-night address — pre-filled above for each country
  • An email you can check — each card's QR is emailed to you

🇦🇺 Coming home to Melbourne: Australia's Incoming Passenger Card is the paper card handed out on the plane — fill it on board, nothing to pre-do.
