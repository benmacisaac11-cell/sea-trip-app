    What~12km jungle gorge, Class II–III rapids, hidden waterfalls, Ramayana cliff carvings only seen from the river. ~2hrs on the water + buffet lunch. Beginner-friendly, runs year-round (dry season fine).
    ✅ Book MASON ADVENTURES (best solo pick)Est. 1989 · 4.5★/1,508 TripAdvisor · longest river run (starts furthest upstream) · no minimum-persons wall. Adult IDR 795k (~AUD 78) incl. free Ubud pickup, gear, insurance, buffet lunch. Book at book.masonadventures.com → 09:00 session, give ARTOTEL Haniman address (Jl. Jatayu 6). Book 2–3 days ahead.

    ⚠️ Hip — the real demandThe put-in/take-out is a long STEEP staircase (~300–500 steps down + climb back up) — the biggest single hip load of the trip. The paddling itself is low-impact (you brace with your feet). Go slow, handrails, grippy shoes, rest on the climb.

  • Budget operators (Bali Bintang etc.) stop mid-river to sell drinks/snacks/photos then pad the bill at reception. Mason & Sobek are tax+service-included — pay up front, carry small cash for the optional mid-river stop, decline the photo package.

The cabaret + ladyboy shows are mainstream tourist entertainment and worth catching once.
